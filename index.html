3D Shapes: Surface Area and Volume

Cube

Surface Area = 6 × a²
Volume = a³

Cuboid

Surface Area = 2(lw + lh + wh)
Volume = l × w × h

Cylinder

Surface Area = 2πr(h + r)
Volume = πr²h

Cone

Surface Area = πr(l + r)
Volume = (1/3)πr²h

Sphere

Surface Area = 4πr²
Volume = (4/3)πr³

Hemisphere

Surface Area = 3πr²
Volume = (2/3)πr³
